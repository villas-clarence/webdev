Vayang Rolling Hills

Wide green slopes with panoramic ocean views ‚Äî a classic spot for photos and sunsets. Gentle walking paths and open grassland create an iconic Batanes scene.

Basco Lighthouse (Naidi Lighthouse)

An iconic lighthouse overlooking Basco town and the coastline. Great for sunrise/sunset views and a sense of orientation on Batan Island.

Valugan Boulder Beach

A dramatic shoreline covered with volcanic boulders shaped by powerful waves. Excellent for dramatic coastal photography and learning about local geology.

Marlboro Country (Racuh a Payaman)

Rolling pastures often compared to famous grassland vistas. Expect wide-open sky views, grazing cows or goats, and pastoral peace.

Mt. Iraya

A dormant volcano visible from parts of Batan Island. Hiking is possible but recommended only for experienced trekkers with a local guide.

Dipnaysupuan Japanese Tunnel

A historic WWII-era tunnel dug during the Japanese occupation. A visit gives insight into Batanes' wartime history and local stories.
