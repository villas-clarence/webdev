Cultural Notes & Travel Tips

  • Respect local customs: Ivatan communities are conservative and welcoming. Always ask before photographing people or private homes.
  • Bring cash: ATMs are limited (mainly in Basco).
  • Pack windproof clothing: Weather can change quickly; winds are strong year-round.
  • Connectivity: Internet and cell signal may be slow or limited. Download offline maps if needed.
  • Accommodations: Options range from homestays to guesthouses. Book early during peak season.
  • Try local food: Sample Ivatan dishes like uvud balls, lunis, and turmeric rice.
  • Practice honesty: Visit the famous Honesty Coffee Shop and experience Ivatan values.
