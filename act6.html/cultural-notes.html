Travel tips & cultural notes

  • Respect local customs — many Ivatan communities are conservative and welcoming. Ask before photographing people and private homes.
  • Bring cash — ATMs are limited (Basco has the main services).
  • Pack windproof clothing and comfortable shoes — wind and sudden weather changes are common.
  • Internet and cell signal may be limited; prepare for slower connectivity and bring offline maps if needed.
  • Accommodations range from simple homestays to small guesthouses. Book ahead during peak season.
