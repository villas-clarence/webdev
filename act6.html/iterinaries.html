How to spend your Vacaton in Batanes

3-Day Weekend (fast highlights)

  1. Day 1 — Arrival & Basco:
    • Arrive in Basco. Check in to lodge/inn.
    • Visit Basco Lighthouse and Basco town proper.
    • Sunset at Vayang Rolling Hills.
  2. Day 2 — Batan East / Marlboro Country:
    • Visit Marlboro Country (rolling hills) and Valugan Boulder Beach.
    • Explore nearby viewpoint and cultural sites; meet local artisans if available.
  3. Day 3 — Sabtang short trip or local immersion:
    • Take a morning boat to Sabtang (weather permitting) to see stone houses and cliffs.
    • Return to Basco for flight home in the afternoon/evening.

4-Day Relaxed (includes Sabtang)

  1. Day 1: Arrive Basco, Basco Lighthouse, local market exploration.
  2. Day 2: Vayang Rolling Hills, Marlboro Country, and Valugan Boulder Beach.
  3. Day 3: Full day Sabtang: Savidug and Chavayan villages, Sabtang cliffs; stay overnight if available.
  4. Day 4: Return to Basco, quick souvenir shopping, depart.

5-Day Adventure (includes Itbayat — adventurous travelers only)

  1. Day 1: Basco arrival, lighthouse, orientation.
  2. Day 2: Vayang, Marlboro Country & Valugan.
  3. Day 3: Boat to Sabtang or local island hopping.
  4. Day 4: (Weather permitting) Trip to Itbayat — dramatic cliffs, traditional village life.
  5. Day 5: Travel back to Basco and depart.
