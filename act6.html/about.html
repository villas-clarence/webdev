About Batanes

Batanes is an archipelago of small islands sitting between Luzon and Taiwan. It is famous for dramatic cliffs, vast open skies, and traditional Ivatan stone houses built to withstand frequent typhoons. Popular natural attractions include Vayang Rolling Hills, Marlboro Country (a gently rolling pasture on Batan Island), Basco Lighthouse, Valugan Boulder Beach, and the sea cliffs on Sabtang and Itbayat. The islands are also a prime spot for sunrise and sunset photography and quiet nature walks.
