About Batanes

Batanes is the northernmost province of the Philippines, known for its rolling hills, dramatic cliffs, and traditional Ivatan stone houses.

The province is composed of 10 islands, with only 3 inhabited: Batan, Sabtang, and Itbayat. Its capital is Basco.

Batanes is famous for its peaceful atmosphere, friendly locals, and breathtaking natural beauty. The unique Ivatan culture and architecture make it a must-visit destination.
